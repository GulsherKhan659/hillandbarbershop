History Since 2010

Our Brand History

Prince & Hill Barber was established in 2010 with one goal in mind: to deliver premium grooming experiences tailored to every individual. Starting as a small shop with a big vision, we've grown into a recognized brand known for blending classic barbering traditions with modern style.

With a passion for precision and personal care, we've built a loyal community that trusts us for professional cuts, clean fades, and authentic grooming services. Every haircut tells a story—and we're proud to be a part of yours.

David Beckham

Mission Mission

Brand Mission

At Prince & Hill Barber, our mission is simple: to empower confidence through expert grooming. We believe that a great haircut goes beyond appearance—it influences how you feel, present, and carry yourself every day.

We are dedicated to delivering top-tier service, fostering a welcoming atmosphere, and staying true to the values of craftsmanship, respect, and style that define who we are.
