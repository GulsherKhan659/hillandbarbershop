Grooming Essentials

Find answers to our most frequently asked questions about services, appointments, and care routines.

How often should I get a haircut?

For most styles, we recommend every 3-4 weeks to maintain sharpness. Fades require more frequent visits (2-3 weeks) while longer styles can go 6-8 weeks.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are strongly recommended to guarantee your preferred time and barber. We accommodate walk-ins based on availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a 50% service fee. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Digital and physical gift cards are available in any amount. Perfect for birthdays, holidays, or treating someone to premium grooming.

What safety measures do you have?

All tools are sterilized between clients. Stations are sanitized after each service. Disposable implements are used where appropriate.

Which products do you recommend?

We carry premium grooming products tailored to different hair types and styles. Our barbers will recommend solutions during your service.
